# Determinant

Definition (brief)
- Scalar value det(A) associated with a square matrix A that encodes invertibility and volume scaling.

Small cases
- 2×2: det([[a,b],[c,d]]) = ad − bc
- 3×3: expansion by minors or rule of Sarrus (brief note)
