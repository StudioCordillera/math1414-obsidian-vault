# Reduced Row Echelon Form (RREF)

Definition
- A matrix is in RREF if it is in REF and additionally every leading entry is 1 and is the only nonzero entry in its column.

Use
- Unique canonical form for a given row-equivalence class; reveals pivot and free variables directly.
