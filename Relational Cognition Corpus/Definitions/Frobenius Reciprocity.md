# Frobenius Reciprocity

Statement
- For f: X→Y with left adjoint f_!: Sub(X)→Sub(Y) and any A∈Sub(X), B∈Sub(Y), we have f_!(A ∧ f^*(B)) ≅ f_!(A) ∧ B.

Interpretation
- Distributes existential quantification over conjunction under substitution; interacts with images and pullbacks.
