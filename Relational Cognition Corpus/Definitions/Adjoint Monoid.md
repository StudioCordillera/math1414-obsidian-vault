# Adjoint Monoid

Type: definition
Tags: node/definition, domain/cat, cat/relations, logic/regular, status/active

Definition
- In a monoidal po-category, an adjoint commutative monoid is a commutative monoid (c, μ, η) such that μ and η are right adjoints; equivalently, the corresponding comonoid maps are their left adjoints.
