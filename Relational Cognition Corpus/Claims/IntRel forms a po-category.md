# Internal Relations form a po-category

Type: claim
Tags: node/claim, domain/cat, cat/relations, logic/regular, status/active

Statement
- For any regular calculus P: FRg(T) → Poset, internal relations IntRel_P(φ1, φ2) with composition defined via wiring (nesting + pullback/image) form a po-category.
