# Fundamental Lemma of Regular Categories

Type: claim
Tags: node/claim, domain/cat, cat/relations, logic/regular, status/active

Statement
- In a regular category R, left adjoints in Rel_R are precisely graphs of morphisms in R. There is an identity-on-objects isomorphism R ≅ LAdj(Rel_R).
