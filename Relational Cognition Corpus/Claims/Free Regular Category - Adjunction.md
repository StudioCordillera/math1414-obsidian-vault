# Free Regular Category Adjunction

Type: claim
Tags: node/claim, domain/cat, cat/relations, logic/regular, status/active

Statement
- FRg(T) is the free regular category on a set T, with adjunction FRg(-) ⊣ Ob. The counit ⟨-⟩: FRg(Ob R) → R is regular.
