# Adjoint Monoids

In Poset:
- Adjoint monoids correspond to meet-semilattices; multiplication is meet ∧ and unit is ⊤.

In Rel_R:
- Each object carries a canonical adjoint monoid structure; morphisms respecting adjoints correspond to functional relations.
