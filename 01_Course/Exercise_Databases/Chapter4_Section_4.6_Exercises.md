## Key Modeling Concepts
- **Exponential Growth:** y = y₀e^{kt} where k > 0
- **Exponential Decay:** y = y₀e^{kt} where k < 0
- **Logistic Growth:** y = $\frac{c}{1 + ae^{-bx}}$
- **Power Models:** y = ax^b
- **Logarithmic Models:** y = a + b ln(x)

## Key Model Types

### Exponential Models
- **Continuous Growth:** A = Pe^{rt}
- **Compound Interest:** A = P(1 + $\frac{r}{n}$)^{nt}
- **Population Growth:** P(t) = P₀e^{kt}
- **Radioactive Decay:** N(t) = N₀e^{-λt}

### Logarithmic Models
- **Learning Curves:** S = a + b ln(t)
- **Magnitude Scales:** M = log($\frac{I}{I₀}$)
- **pH and Chemistry:** pH = -log[H⁺]

### Logistic Models
- **Population with Limits:** P(t) = $\frac{L}{1 + ae^{-kt}}$
- **Market Penetration:** N(t) = $\frac{M}{1 + ae^{-rt}}$
