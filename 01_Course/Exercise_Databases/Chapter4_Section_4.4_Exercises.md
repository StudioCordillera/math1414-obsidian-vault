## Learning Objectives

## Key Properties
- **Product Property:** log_b(xy) = log_b x + log_b y
- **Quotient Property:** log_b($\frac{x}{y}$) = log_b x − log_b y
- **Power Property:** log_b x^p = p log_b x
- **Change of Base:** log_b x = $\frac{\log_a x}{\log_a b}$
